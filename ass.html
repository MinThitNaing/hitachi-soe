After Sale Services

HISEM would like to provide two kinds of aftersales service for installed transformers such as one time inspection service and annual service contract to all customers around Myanmar. There are three types of After Sale Service provided by HISEM which are

  • (1) Site Survey Service
  • (2) One Time Inspection Service
  • (3) Yearly Maintenance Contract Service

After Sale Services

After Sale Services

  • Site Survey Service

  • One Time Inspection Service

  • Yearly Maintenance Service Contract

Site Survey Service

Upon on call requested by customer, HISEM inspects Transformer and Panel onsite under operation condition. The following check point for Routine Inspection of site survey service are mentioned. .

  • Temperature and Load of Transformer
  • Oil Level
  • Noise (Sound Level)
  • Lose Connection
  • Pressure Relief Device
  • Bushing
  • Tap Changer
  • Breather
  • Buchholz Relay (Gas Relay)

One Time Inspection Service

Under the One Time Inspection Service, the following service scopes are included.

  • Cleaning Process to overall
  • Inspection to all components on its
  • Site Test (Insulation Resistance Test/Turn Ratio Measurement/ Winding Resistance Measurement)
  • Measurement of Earthing Resistance
  • Oil Analysis (Dielectric Breakdown Voltage/ Water Content/ Dielectric Dissipation Factor)
  • Tighten Check to all Connection
  • According to one time inspection service result, if we need to replace material and spare parts, HISEM will charge for service charges and the additional cost for replacement materials to the customers

Yearly Maintenance Service Contract

After customer makes yearly maintenance service contract with HISEM, HISEM provides two times of aftersales services within one year; first time inspection process and second time maintenance process.

First Time Periodical Inspection Process

HISEM will check customer’s transformer for after sales service s in first 6 month of contracted year. The following service scopes are mentioned.

  • Cleaning Process to overall
  • Inspection to all components on its.
  • Site Test (Insulation Resistance Test/Turn Ratio Measurement/ Winding Resistance Measurement)
  • Measurement of Earthing Resistance
  • Oil Analysis (Dielectric Breakdown Voltage/ Water Content/ Dielectric Dissipation Factor)
  • Tighten Check to all connection.

Second Time Preventive Maintenance Process

Based on the report of first-time inspection, HISEM provides the following service again and replace the items as needed.

  • Cleaning Process to overall
  • Inspection to all components on its
  • Site Test (Insulation Resistance Test/Turn Ratio Measurement/ Winding Resistance Measurement)
  • Measurement of Earthing Resistance
  • Oil Analysis (Dielectric Breakdown Voltage/ Water Content/ Dielectric Dissipation Factor)
  • Tighten Check to all connection

There are no additional service charges for any service call or unexcepted breakdown case within contracted year. Customers will be charged again for replacement parts as per inspection results.
